# Bloxco Stores Rules & Guidelines

_Last Updated: November 4, 2024_  
_Updated By: CookieCandle_

Welcome to the Bloxco Stores community! This document outlines the rules and guidelines for participating in our Discord server and other services.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:**  
> We reserve the right to moderate actions we deem necessary. This is not an exhaustive list. Use common sense when interacting within the Bloxco community. These rules may be updated at any time.

---

## 📜 Section 1 – Follow Discord’s Terms of Service & Community Guidelines

<details>
<summary>1.1 Discord TOS Compliance</summary>

We enforce all of Discord’s [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).  
Claiming to be under 13 will result in an immediate ban from the Bloxco Discord server.

</details>

---

## 🕹️ Section 2 – Follow Roblox’s TOS & Community Standards

<details>
<summary>2.1 Roblox TOS Compliance</summary>

As Bloxco is hosted on the Roblox platform, all community members must follow Roblox’s [Terms of Use](https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004647846) and [Community Standards](https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/203313410).

</details>

<details>
<summary>2.2 No Exploits or Hacks</summary>

Use or promotion of exploits, cheats, or hacks is strictly prohibited and will result in punishment.

</details>

---

## 📢 Section 3 – No Advertising or Promotions

<details>
<summary>3.1 No Server or Game Promotion</summary>

Advertising is not allowed. This includes sending links to Roblox games or other services for promotion.

</details>

<details>
<summary>3.2 No DM Advertising</summary>

Direct message (DM) advertising is strictly prohibited. Violations will be handled with appropriate punishments.

</details>

---

## 🤝 Section 4 – Respect All Members

<details>
<summary>4.1 General Respect</summary>

All members are expected to be respectful toward others within the community.

</details>

<details>
<summary>4.2 Zero Tolerance for Hate</summary>

Racism, homophobia, or any form of hate speech will result in an immediate and permanent ban from all Bloxco services.

</details>

---

## 🔒 Section 5 – Do Not Bypass AutoMod

<details>
<summary>5.1 AutoMod Protection</summary>

Our AutoMod system is in place to keep the server safe and civil. Do not attempt to bypass it.

</details>

<details>
<summary>5.2 Filter Bypass</summary>

Avoid using special characters or formatting tricks to evade moderation filters—such attempts will be moderated as violations.

</details>

---

## 👤 Section 6 – No Alternative Accounts

<details>
<summary>6.1 Alt Account Ban Policy</summary>

Do not use alternative accounts to evade moderation actions. Both your main and alt accounts will be banned if detected.

</details>

---

## 📚 Section 7 – Keep Content Appropriate

<details>
<summary>7.1 PG-13 Content Standard</summary>

All server discussions should remain appropriate for a PG-13 audience.

</details>

<details>
<summary>7.2 NSFW is Prohibited</summary>

NSFW content is never allowed and should not be shared or discussed within the community.

</details>

---

## 🔇 Section 8 – No Discussion of Moderation

<details>
<summary>8.1 Respect Moderation Decisions</summary>

To keep the environment drama-free, do not publicly discuss any moderation actions from either the game or Discord server.

</details>

---

## 🚫 Section 9 – No Drama or Harmful Topics

<details>
<summary>9.1 No Antagonistic Behavior</summary>

Do not join the server with the intention of stirring up conflict or targeting others.

</details>

<details>
<summary>9.2 Sensitive Topics are Prohibited</summary>

Avoid discussing topics such as terrorism, war, politics, or violence. These can be deeply upsetting and are not suitable for the Bloxco community.

</details>

---

## ✅ Summary

These guidelines are here to keep the Bloxco community safe, respectful, and fun for everyone.

> For questions or clarification, please contact a moderator or check the help channels in the server.
